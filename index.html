About Hope For Strays

A small rescue effort with a big mission.

We work on the ground to help stray dogs facing hunger, injury, abandonment, accidents, and medical emergencies. Every rescue takes time, effort, and community support.

Many street dogs suffer silently because help arrives too late. Hope For Strays exists to act faster — with feeding, treatment, foster care, adoption support, and emergency response whenever possible.

FAQ

Questions people may ask before helping

How can I donate?

You can donate through UPI, QR, or bank transfer. Add your payment details in the Donate section before publishing.

Can I adopt a rescued dog?

Yes. We support responsible adoption for rescued puppies and dogs. Contact us on WhatsApp to know who is available.

How do I report an injured dog?

Use the WhatsApp or call button and share the location, photo, and condition of the dog for faster help.

Can I volunteer?

Yes. Volunteers can help with feeding, transport, rescue coordination, awareness, and adoption support.
